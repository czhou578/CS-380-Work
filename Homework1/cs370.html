CS 370 Internet of Things (IoT)

Explores the possible applications of connecting everyday appliances to the internet. Gives students an opportunity to construct their own IoT application.

  • I hope to learn how a device can be connected using wifi to a local or broad network.
  • I also hope to learn the possible ways that this new technology can impact the world in the future.
  • Lastly, I want to see how my knowledge from Computer Networking class is applied in a hands on activity.
